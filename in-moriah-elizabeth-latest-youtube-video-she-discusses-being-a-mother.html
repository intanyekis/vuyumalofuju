In Moriah Elizabeth Latest YouTube Video, She Discusses Being A Mother

Toy with a soft texture An influential YouTuber named Moriah Elizabeth recently shared some startling news. The well-known content creator admitted to being a mother in front of admirers.

Since the 27-year-old had kept the child and pregnancy off of social media, her followers were taken aback. The emotional video’s sole parent admits that her kid is 10 months old. There’s Something I Want to Tell You… has almost two million views as of the time of writing. During the first minute of the short clip, Moriah Elizabeth revealed…

I’m a mom, she said.

She continued:

The only child I have is a hidden daughter. “Nothing insane exists.”

The woman revealed the age of her daughter and said that viewers were ignorant of the great secret because she had been streaming videos on her 7.5 million-subscriber channel during her pregnancy. Regarding her decision to be filmed throughout her pregnancy, Elizabeth said:

“My most recent pregnancy video featured a makeover from the dollar store.” This film was created while I was eight months pregnant. I raised my table quite a little to hide my pregnancy from view.

Additionally, Moriah Elizabeth asserted that during her pregnancy, she only missed one week of blogging. The mother claimed that while going through labor, she urged her husband to tell her followers that she was unwell and unable to post. The YouTuber also gave a justification for holding off on publishing the birth announcement. Elizabeth Moriah proclaimed:

“I put it off because I was afraid of how you guys would respond if I disclosed sooner,” the speaker said. I didn’t want you to believe that I was trying to trick you. I hope I haven’t infuriated you. I hope it’s clear to you why I waited so long to inform you.

Elizabeth continued by stating that she would rarely post videos of her daughter on social media in order to protect the privacy of the child and that she didn’t want to disclose her child’s identity. After that, the YouTuber added her daughter to the clip.

YouTube channel of Moriah is highly recognized for her makeover and plush toy videos

The creator of the content started her YouTube channel in August 2010. She got her to start filming entertaining vlog-style videos. Later, Moriah shifted her attention to producing DIY films and squishy toys. She gained notoriety when her television show Wreck This Journal became a hit.

She has gained fans thanks to her unique communication style. Her clients can send her their squishes, which she will paint and modify. She fixes broken soft toys in addition to painting the ones that need them.

After establishing herself as a well-known artist on the video-sharing platform, she released her book, Create This Book. She has stated that her mother is Caucasian and that her father is Peruvian-Italian, although she hasn’t identified where she was born in America. Jordan was the man she married at the age of 19.
