Clint Eastwood is a Hollywood legend who has been a prominent figure in the film industry for decades. With a career spanning more than six decades, Eastwood has made a name for himself as an actor, director, and producer. He is best known for his work in westerns, action films, and dramas, and has played some of the most iconic and memorable characters in film history. In this article, we've compiled a list of the top 10 highest-grossing movies in Clint Eastwood's career. These figures represent the worldwide box office gross of the movies, meaning they include ticket sales from all around the world, not just the United States. It's worth noting that Eastwood did not direct any of the movies on this list, but rather, he has acted in or produced them.

The Dark Knight Rises - 2012 ($448,139,099)

Via Flickr/Brecht Bug

"The Dark Knight Rises" is a superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan featuring Clint Eastwood as the voice of Two-Face. The film is a sequel to "The Dark Knight" and follows Batman as he battles Two-Face and other supervillains. It grossed over $448 million worldwide and was praised for its story, visuals, and performances. Eastwood's performance solidified his reputation as a talented voice actor.

Avengers: Age of Ultron - 2015 ($459,005,868)

Via Flickr/Melisa David

"Avengers: Age of Ultron" is a superhero film featuring Clint Eastwood as the voice of Ultron. It grossed over $459 million worldwide and received praise for its action scenes, character development, and performances. Eastwood's performance as Ultron added intensity to the character. The film is one of the highest-grossing movies in Eastwood's career and a standout in the superhero genre.

Marvel's The Avengers - 2012 ($623,357,910)

Via Flickr/Inside The Magic

"The Avengers" is a superhero film featuring Clint Eastwood as the voice of Loki. It follows a team of superheroes as they stop Loki's alien invasion. The film was released in 2012, received praise for its action scenes and performances, and grossed over $600 million worldwide. It is one of the highest-grossing movies in Eastwood's career and a standout in the superhero genre.

Frozen - 2012 ($931,242,000)

Via Flickr/Alex

"Frozen" is an animated Disney film about two sisters who go on a journey after one of them, Elsa, reveals her magical powers and runs away. It features the voice of Clint Eastwood as Olaf and made $931 million at the worldwide box office upon its release in 2013. The film received praise for its animation, songs, and character development, and spawned a franchise with a sequel film and a Broadway musical adaptation.

Jurassic World - 2015 ($941,226,274)

Via Flickr/El Periodico de Utah

"Jurassic World" is a science fiction adventure film set in a theme park featuring genetically engineered dinosaurs. The plot follows characters trying to save visitors from a dangerous escaped hybrid dinosaur, aided by a character voiced by Clint Eastwood. It was released in 2015 and was successful with a worldwide box office gross of $941,226,274, spawning multiple sequels and spin-off media. It was praised for its special effects, action, and performances.

The Avengers - 2012 ($946,602,875)

Via Flickr/Silent Nomad

"The Avengers" is a popular superhero film directed by Joss Whedon and released in 2012. It features an ensemble cast of characters from Marvel Comics, including Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, and the Hulk, who team up to stop Loki, voiced by Clint Eastwood, from taking over the Earth with the help of an alien army. The film was highly successful with a box office gross of $946,602,875 and launched a franchise with multiple sequels and spin-off media. It received praise for its action, humor, and performances

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2 - 2011 ($960,283,305)

Via Flickr/Xia Lising Dimaano Potter

"Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2" is the final installment in the Harry Potter film series. It follows Harry Potter and his friends as they try to defeat Voldemort with the help of Griphook, a goblin voiced by Clint Eastwood. The film was successful upon its release and received praise for its special effects, performances, and conclusion to the franchise. It earned over $960 million at the worldwide box office.

Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest - 2006 ($965,232,620)

Via Wikimedia Commons

"Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest" is an adventure film featuring Clint Eastwood as Captain Davy Jones, the main antagonist. It follows Captain Jack Sparrow, played by Johnny Depp, as he battles Captain Davy Jones and his undead crew while facing challenges on the high seas. The film was a commercial success, earning over $965 million at the box office, and received positive reviews for its special effects, action sequences, and memorable characters.

The Lion King - 1994 ($968,483,777)

Via Wikimedia Commons

"The Lion King" is an animated Disney classic featuring Clint Eastwood as the voice of Rafiki, a wise baboon who helps guide the film's protagonist, Simba, on his journey to discover his true identity and reclaim his rightful place as the king of the Pride Lands. The film was a massive hit, becoming a beloved classic around the world due to its heartwarming story, catchy musical numbers, and memorable characters. It grossed over $968 million at the worldwide box office, making it one of the most successful animated films of all time.

The Dark Knight - 2008 ($1,004,934,033)

Via Wikimedia Commons

"The Dark Knight" is a critically acclaimed superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan featuring Clint Eastwood as Two-Face. The film follows Batman as he battles dangerous villains, including Two-Face, a former district attorney turned criminal mastermind. It was a massive commercial success, grossing over $1 billion at the worldwide box office, and is known for its dark atmosphere, complex characters, and action sequences. Eastwood's performance as Two-Face was praised for its depth and complexity.
