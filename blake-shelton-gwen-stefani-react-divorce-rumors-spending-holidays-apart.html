Blake Shelton And Gwen Stefani React To Divorce Rumors After Spending Holidays Apart

Fans of Blake Shelton and Gwen Stefani have been concerned after it was revealed the couple spent the holidays apart. However, the duo has responded to the speculation to reassure the public that all is well in their marriage, despite reports.

On Friday, Gwen revealed to her Instagram followers that she’d been inducted into Orange County’s first Hall of Fame. Her post showed that Shelton attended the event alongside her to celebrate the big career milestone.

Blake Supported Gwen During Her Big Career Milestone

“Back in the 7-1-4 days, i’ll always be an Orange County girl,” Stefanie wrote on X (formerly Twitter). “Thank you to the County of Orange for the incredible honor + induction to first ever the Hall of Fame !!”

She accompanied the post with video footage from the day. It began showing the “No Doubt” singer wearing a denim outfit, featuring a ruffled skirt, matching boots, and a button-down shirt. Later in the video, Gwen could be seen walking hand-in-hand with Shelton as she walked to the event, where she later gave a heartfelt acceptance speech when presented with the award on stage.

Gwen didn’t address the recent speculation over her marriage directly, but Blake’s appearance and their sweet moments of PDA in the video seem to dispel any notions that things are wrong in their years-long relationship.

Gwen And Blake Have Been Dodging Divorce Rumors For Months

Last month, fans were surprised to learn Blake and Gwen were planning to spend the holidays apart due to work obligations, something Stefani admitted she was totally alright with.

Speaking to Access Hollywood, Gwen said that she was originally planning to be in the audience at her husband’s New Year’s Eve event in Nashville. But she was given a last-minute opportunity to do her own show in Las Vegas, which Stefani says she couldn’t pass up.

"Well, it's funny because I was going to be at his show, and then Vegas called and wanted me to be in Vegas," Gwen, 54, told the outlet.

"Last year I did Vegas and I was like, 'Remind me to never do Vegas again.' [But] you sometimes want to... not be the host," the pop star continued. "So I was like, 'Eh, I'm gonna do it.' If you're gonna be working then I'm gonna be working — so I'm just gonna fly in and out it's gonna be awesome. I'm really looking forward to it."

Gwen’s admission came after months of divorce speculation, as various reports claimed the couple were going through a rough patch after tying the knot in 2021.

“Times are tough for Gwen and Blake,” a source revealed in July. “They’re spending a lot of time apart and the relationship is suffering. Friends are worried that if they don’t address their problems before they spiral out of control, divorce might be in the cards for them.”

“There isn’t much for them to talk about,” the insider continued. “She can put on cowboy boots and sing a country duet with Blake, but Gwen doesn’t really fit in that whole country lifestyle. When she’s in Oklahoma, she misses L.A. and all its conveniences.”

Even if Stefani and Shelton are facing martial problems, their recent public appearance seems to suggest they’re maintaining a united front.
