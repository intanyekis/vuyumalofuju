'Welcome to Sweetie Pie's' Robbie Montgomery Defies Her Age in a Colorful Robe &amp; Yellow Shoes

July 07, 2024
blog

At 80, Robbie Montgomery is defying all the odds to look her best despite being engulfed in a sea of family troubles. The icon shared a photo of herself looking ageless in an eye-catching outfit.

“Welcome To Sweetie Pie” star Robbie Montgomery marked Mother’s Day in style. The 80-year-old ushered in the big day with a powerful post on Instagram.

Montgomery shared a picture on Instagram, which featured her donning a white top over matching pants as she rocked her age-defying looks.

Robbie Montgomery attends Essence's 9th annual Black Women In Hollywood event on February 25, 2016 | Photo: Getty Images

She paired the combination with a colorful robe sporting yellow string details on the lower edge, which matched her spiny yellow flat shoes.

The soul singer complemented her stunning appearance with a beautiful smile which noticeably took years off her age. She posed at the bottom of a long flight of stairs, with her left hand in her pocket. Captioning the post, the icon wrote:

“A mother’s love is the purest love you’ll ever know. Happy Mother’s Day!!”

Her candid post attracted over a thousand comments and several likes from fans, all joining in the remarkable celebration. Many commended the star’s beauty and how youthful she looked in the pic.

The reality star’s epic Mother’s Day quote captured many hearts for good reasons. More than being a mom, Montgomery’s journey as a mother has been plagued with pitfalls. Yet, the icon’s big heart remains open to her offspring.

Fans offered words of consolation to the legendary songstress, encouraging her to remain steadfast.

One of the biggest tragedies that hit the family came in 2016 when her grandson, Andre Montgomery, was shot dead in a murder-for-hire plot.

The restaurateur believed the 21-year-old’s death to be a setup, as he received a call from an unknown person asking him to go outside seconds before the shooting.

Almost two decades before Andre’s death, his father, Andre Sr., also died in a Gangland attack which made the news of the youngster’s demise more heartbreaking.

Last year, authorities arrested the reality star’s second son, Tim Norman, in connection with the murder plot that claimed Andre’s life. However, the 41-year-old pleaded not guilty and demanded to be let out of jail to spend time with his mom while awaiting trial.

The presiding judge denied his request. Following Norman's arrest, fans offered words of consolation to the legendary songstress, encouraging her to remain steadfast. She released a statement shortly after, thanking fans for their support.

Before Norman’s arrest, he was involved in a legal entanglement with his mom following a 2016 lawsuit she filed against him over trademark infringement and misappropriation of funds.

Despite their previous legal entanglements, Montgomery and her son upheld their familial ties above all else, cherishing their love for each other.
