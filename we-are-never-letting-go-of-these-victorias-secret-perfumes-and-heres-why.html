We Are Never Letting Go Of These Victorias Secret Perfumes, And Heres Why

August 05, 2024
blog

So you're undoubtedly thinking - Victoria's Secret perfumes? You are not alone. While Victoria's Secret is best known for its intimate apparel, including bras, lingerie, and pajamas, the brand is also a major player in the perfume industry. Amazingly, one of the best selling perfumes in the United States is Victoria's Secret Bombshell Eau de parfum.

However, suppose you're looking for something other than Bombshell. In that case, there's a wide variety of beautiful perfumes out there. The best Victoria's Secret fragrances will make you feel a certain way, regardless of your character. They have various scented veils of mist that are sure to please everybody. If you want to try something new or refresh your current trademark scent, we've gathered a list of Victoria's Secret's top-rated fragrances for your convenience.

10 Bombshell Eau de Parfum: The Best Overall

Bombshell is an eternal favorite for many, a subtle fragrance with undertones of Shangri-La Peony, Purple Passion Fruit, and Vanilla Orchid. This fruity and floral scent is a favorite among young women and girls of all ages, likely due to the fact that it is not overpoweringly potent.

  • Shop:amazon.com

9 Victoria's Secret Love: Ideal For Daily Use

Victoria's Secret Love was motivated by the idea of the cozy, pleasant aroma of a partner's t-shirt. Despite being described as a "fresh" scent, the combination of apricot, woods, musk, and juniper gives it a gentle, warm, skin-like character that is both romantic and calming. In addition, it is more subtle than other well-liked VS fragrances, making it a good choice for those who value delicacy in their perfume.

  • Shop:amazon.com

8 Rapture Eau de Cologne: The Best Spicy Fragrance

Victoria's Secret Rapture Eau de Cologne debuted in 1992 and has been retired and revived multiple times. Orange flowers, amber, citrus fruits, and jasmine come together to create a refreshing, spicy, and pleasurable scent for women. The top notes of the cologne are orange blossom and citrus, and the middle notes are jasmine, Bulgarian rose, and freesia.

  • Shop:amazon.com

7 Victoria's Secret Heavenly: The Best Sweet Scent

One of the first Victoria's Secret fragrances, Heavenly, was created as a tribute to the brand's original angels. It's like bottled confidence and oozes a warm, sensual, and dazzling scent. With notes of sweet vanilla, heavy sandalwood, velvety musk, and a hint of peony, Heavenly is sophisticated and glamorous.

  • Shop:amazon.com

6 Victoria's Secret Very Sexy: The Best Fruity Fragrance

Very Sexy conveys assurance and confidence. Robust flavors like ripe clementine and sweet berries are at the forefront of the concentrated juice, but a pleasant woodsy aroma develops on the skin after a while. It's an attention-getting fragrance due to the sophistication it exudes, together with its provocative and complex qualities.

  • Shop:amazon.com

5 Victoria’s Secret Tease Crème Cloud: The Best Gourmand

Tease Crème Cloud is the dreamy little sister of the original Tease. Yummy whipped vanilla meringue, santal flower, amber, and creamy musk all contribute to the light gourmand perfume that is sweet and lively. Similar to a sweet treat.

  • Shop:amazon.com

4 So In Love Eau de Parfum: Suitable for All Ages

Like many other perfumes that have come back due to customer demand, So In Love is an all-time classic that never goes out of style and can be worn by women of any age. This fragrance is delightful without being sickeningly sweet and features floral elements, including rose, violet leaves, cognac, jasmine, honey, and ylang-ylang. So In Love, a perfume that doesn't smell fruity, has been popular since it was released.

  • Shop:amazon.com

3 Victoria's Secret Bombshell Intense: Best Fruity Floral

You may think of Bombshell Intense, the fragrance, as the olfactory version of the color red. When you combine tart cherry, bright peony, and creamy vanilla, you get a chypre fruity floral fragrance that is both powerful and delicious. This trendy fragrance is known for its whimsical nature, which is emphasized by the bottle's vivid red color.

  • Shop:amazon.com

2 Victoria's Secret Very Sexy Sea: Best Citrus

Very Sexy Sea is a beautiful fragrance to try if you have a thing for the smell of the sea and all things blue. The sights and sounds of the Amalfi Coast are captured in Very Sexy Sea. Think of the contrast between the warm glow of tanned skin and the chilly blue of the Mediterranean. Italian Bergamot and opulent Neroli Blossom, both citrus fruits, serve as the fragrance's opening salvo. Sundrenched Cedar's enveloping heat is awash in a wave of cool water. As you inhale this aroma, take yourself to a tranquil beach getaway.

  • Shop:amazon.com

1 Love Spell Eau de Toilette: The Best Mild Fragrance

Victoria's Secret Love Spell is closer to an Eau de Toilette than an Eau de Parfum, thanks to its light undertones of peach, cherry blossom, and white jasmine. For some who find stronger fragrances to be too much to handle daily, Love Spell is a beautiful alternative. However, the scent may remain shorter than an EDP due to a lower concentration. However, Victoria's Secret Love Spell is a great EDT because it lets you enjoy the aroma of flowers for several hours without having to reapply the fragrance.

  • Shop:amazon.com
