Who is Eddie Huang?

Eddie Huang is an American famous restaurateur, chef, and food famous television.

He is well-known as a writer and attorney. He is also the owner of the BaoHaus, a sandwich shop.

What is the birth name of Eddie Huang?

He was born on the 1st of March, 1982 in Washington D.C. His birth name is Edwyn Charles Huang.

He was born to parents Louis Huang and Jessica Huanghe. His Taiwanese immigrant parents raised him.

He has two siblings: Evan Huang and Emery Huang and they all were raised in Silver Spring, Maryland.

Eddie Huang holds American citizenship and his ethnicity is Asian.

Education, School/College University

In 2004, Eddie Huang attended Rollins College and earned a B.A. degree in English and Film from Rollins College.

Later, he joined the University of Pittsburgh and received a J.D. (Juris Doctor degree) from the Cardozo School of Law at Yeshiva University in 2008.

Eddie Huang: Professional Life, Career

He started his career as a lawyer working in corporate law at Chadbourne & Parke in New York City. In addition, he worked as a Summer Associate in 2006 and 2007 and then started doing stand-up comedy.

Later, Eddie Huang started working as a stand-up comedian and marijuana dealer after the financial crisis of 2007-2008.

Also, he worked as a clothing designer and started his company named ‘Hoodman’ clothing. He used to create clothing designs for his company along with designer Ning Juang.

Eddie Huang learned cooking from his mom and from other chefs. Later, he opened the restaurant BaoHaus in 2009 and then opened another restaurant named Xiao Ye. Later, he owned the Baozi restaurant and worked there for many years.

Also, he was named to the Show 13 list of influential people in food due to his endeavors in the restaurant business in 2013.

In addition, Eddie Huang wrote a book named ‘Fresh Off the Boat’ and later published a book with the same name. His book became one of the best-selling books in the US.

Awards, Nominations

While studying in college, Eddie Huang won the Barbara Lawrence Alfond English Award and the Zora Neale Hurston Award.
