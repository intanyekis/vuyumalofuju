Are '90s Stars Matthew Lawrence And TLC's Chilli Really Dating?

Celebrity couples often come and go, but despite many of them not working out, we can't help but talk about them, and learn how they came together. Many of these couples are completely unexpected, which makes their romance all the more interesting. Of course, there are some that work out in the long run, but not every couple can be like Kurt Russell and Goldie Hawn.

Boy Meets World star, Matthew Lawrence, is no stranger to having his career and love life covered by the media. Whether it's for his wealth, his time with Cheryl Burke, or their subsequent split, Lawrence has remained in the headlines for years.

Recently, Lawrence's love life was the topic of conversation once again, and this time, it was due to being linked to Chilli from TLC. Let's hear what Lawrence had to say about their new romance!

Matthew Lawrence Was A Major Star In The '90s And Early 2000s, With Movies Like 'Mrs. Doubtfire' To His Name

Back in the day, the Lawrence brothers were everywhere. These guys were in movies, TV shows, and commercials, and Matthew Lawrence, the middle brother, was enjoying a thriving career.

On the big screen, Lawrence has been in movies like Mrs. Doubtfire, Kiki's Delivery Service, and The Hot Chick.

His efforts are TV were even more impressive. Lawrence landed roles on shows like Dynasty, Blossom, Brotherly Love, Boy Meets World, and more. Boy Meets World remains arguably his greatest project, and even now, people love re-watching the show, and seeing his character develop a relationship with the rest of the cast.

Lawrence has dished about his time on Bot Meets World, even revealing some hilarious stories.

While on a podcast, Lawrence spoke with co-host and Backstreet Boy, AJ McLean, bringing up a story involving an old boy band rivalry.

"I was a fan of your band. In particular, your voice was the peak of the band by far," said Lawrence.

"We had a dance-off every Friday night that N’SYNC would come to our show where the boys would do ‘I Want It That Way’ in front of the audience just to zing it to N’SYNC. It became a thing," he continued.

Lawrence, of course, is only part of the equation, and the other half is someone that music lovers around the globe should be familiar with.

Chilli Was One Of The Members Of TLC, A Legendary Music Group

At their peak, few groups around were capable of doing what TLC could do, both in the studio, and live on stage. They were everywhere, and Chilli was an integral part of the group's success.

"According to Warner Music Japan, TLC sold over 65,000,000 records (albums and singles) worldwide, including 23,000,000 in the United States and 660,000 in the United Kingdom. The best-selling album by TLC is CRAZYSEXYCOOL, which sold over 14,355,000 copies," Best Selling Albums reports.

After the tragic passing of Left Eye, things were never the same for the group, and the remaining members would try their hand at a variety of things, always carrying their time in the group with them.

Chilli has since taken on a number of different endeavors. She has recorded her own solo music, has acted, and has even played a part in the fight against cyberbullying, something that affected her family.

Now that both parties have been formally introduced, let's take a look at what could be brewing between the two.

Matthew Lawrence Recently Confirmed That He Is Dating Chilli

After being spotted together, rumors swirled of a romance brewing between the two. Thankfully, these rumors proved true, and Lawrence himself has opened up about what's going on between the two.

Said Lawrence , "Life is always a surprise. My life is in a complete bloom right now. I get to spend time with an amazing woman like Chilli. I've never gotten to be able to experience that kind of relationship before, so it's quite special. She's a really, really special human being. I wish more people on the planet were like her. We'd be much better off."

He would give ET more when speaking with them.

"She's just got, you know, her morals and her values. The way she treats her family. She loves my family, so important. We get along. We'll talk on the phone and she's like, 'My mom's calling me.' I'm like, 'Go get it. I know how important that is.' Same thing like my mom calls me. It's not like, 'Why are you talking to your mom?!' It's like, 'Oh, your mom. Go talk to your mom,' you know,'" he continued.

In that same interview, Lawrence opened up about how they met, and how well things are going for the pair.
