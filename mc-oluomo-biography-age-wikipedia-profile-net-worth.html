MC Oluomo Biography

Musiliu Ayinde Akinsanya, born hMarch 14, 1975, better known as MC Oluomo, is a Nigerian businessman and chairman of the National Union of Road Transport Workers (NURTW), Lagos.

MC Oluomo was born in Agbo-Ile Atere area of Oshodi, Lagos State where he had his elementary education.

He became the chairman of NURTW after Tajudeen Agbede earned promotion as the national vice president in Abuja.

Early Life and Education

MC Oluomo grew up in his hometown, Agbo-Ile Atere area of Oshodi, Lagos State, had his primary education in an undisclosed school.

He also obtained senior secondary certificate in Lagos State before he began his journey into business.

He has slight political affiliation with the All Progressive Congress (APC), as he had been seen in the party meeting in few occasions.

Career

MC Oluomo is the current chairman of National Union of Road Transport Workers (NURTW), Lagos State, a position he resumed in October 2019.

Prior to his appointment as NURTW chairman, he was the association’s treasurer and chairman of the Oshodi branch.

MC Oluomo released a book titled “My Service to Humanity” which highlighted his success story and services in Lagos State.
