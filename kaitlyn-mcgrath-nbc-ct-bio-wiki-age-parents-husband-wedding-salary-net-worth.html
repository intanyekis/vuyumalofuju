Kaitlyn McGrath NBC Connecticut News Meteorologist

McGrath is a well-known meteorologist who has been giving accurate and timely forecasts for the weekend evening for NBC Connecticut News since October 2015. Prior to joining NBC, Kaitlyn McGrath had a job as a ekday evening and weekend morning weather anchor for WCSC-TV Live 5 News.
