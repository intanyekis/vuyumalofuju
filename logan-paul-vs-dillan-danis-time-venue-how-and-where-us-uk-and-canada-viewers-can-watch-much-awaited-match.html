Logan Paul vs Dillan Danis: Time, venue and how to watch much-awaited match

May 27, 2024
blog

CLEVELAND, OHIO: The Logan Paul vs Dillon Danis boxing match has been highly anticipated across various social media platforms. Set to take place on October 14, 2023, in Manchester, England, the event falls under the DAZN x Misfits boxing PPV.

Leading up to the match, Danis has stoked controversy by targeting Paul's family. Specifically, he focused on Paul's fiancée, Nina Agdal, sharing explicit pictures of her with her previous partners and even creating manipulated images of her.

Danis didn't stop there; he also aimed insults at Paul's brother, Jake Paul, and made disparaging remarks about their mother. Despite facing legal action, Danis continues to post inappropriate pictures of Agdal regularly.

Logan Paul and Dillon Danis’ bout time and location

In the lead-up to the anticipated bout, the boxing match is scheduled to be the co-main event of the KSI vs Tommy Fury fight this Saturday (October 14).

This will mark Paul's first official boxing match since his 2021 exhibition with Floyd Mayweather, which ended in a no-contest.

On the other hand, this match will be Danis' inaugural foray into the world of boxing. Additionally, it signifies his return to combat sports after a hiatus of over four years. His last appearance was in Bellator in 2019, where he showcased his renowned jiu-jitsu skills.

The timing of the Logan Paul vs Dillon Danis fight will vary depending on the location of fans worldwide. The event is set to take place at the Manchester Arena in England.

However, only a limited number of tickets remain available for in-person attendance. Consequently, fans may opt for the pay-per-view option provided by DAZN.

The fight is anticipated to kick off at approximately 7 PM in the UK. For viewers in America, this translates to 2 PM ET, and the same time applies in Canada.

As for the Paul vs Danis showdown, no official schedule has been released. However, the ring entrances are projected to occur around 10:05 PM in the UK, equivalent to 5:05 PM ET in America and Canada.

Logan Paul weighs in ahead of match against Dillon Danis

After the impending showdown between Logan Paul and Dillon Danis, Paul took to his official Instagram account to share a series of updates.

In one of his IG stories, Paul revealed his pre-match weight, currently tipping the scales at 194.3 pounds (88 kg). This is a standard procedure in combat sports like boxing or MMA, where fighters are required to meet specific weight limits to ensure fair competition.

Weigh-ins typically happen before the actual match to verify that both fighters are within the designated weight range for their respective weight class.

Upon seeing the number, Paul joyfully raised his hands in a victory gesture, wearing a beaming smile. Those around him were visibly elated, offering their support and cheers. Further, paul wrote in the story saying, 'made weight.'

In a subsequent post on Logan Paul's Instagram stories, he shared an image containing more information about his upcoming schedule.

The image featured Logan Paul's message stating, "Fight starts tomorrow at these times." Additionally, the story encouraged viewers to tune in on DAZN.com.
